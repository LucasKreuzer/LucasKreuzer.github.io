.Pirates and the golden bear loot

by anonymous creators.




There once existed two menacing pirates near a bay, seeking refuge in the greenery in Berkeley. The pirates set out on an adventure to scope out a good site for burying their treasures. They nonchalantly traversed the "Cal" campus with a treasure chest and shovel in hand, and settled by the creek, hidden from sight.

Treasure chest and shovel in hand, now settled in the creek, hidden from sight, they buried the chest. The contents of the chest; a brass pirate spy glass, a compass, shells from faraway seas, gifts wrapped in ivy leaves, gold nuggets, handmade custom labeled pirate hats and more.



Anonymous writer, 'Pirates and the golden bear loot', aknownspace, 2020, 1, 9
