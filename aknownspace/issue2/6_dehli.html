.Love in the times of Delhi winters

by Tanya, find her here.




1. On this gloomy day with lack of air and breathlessness,

You decide to be my safety mask.


2. I can't see the buildings behind our house,

The smog is such.

But I can see through your heart pure and clear.


3. We used to cross our paths everyday,

But nowadays I cant find you...

Your number is odd

And mine is even...


4. Roses are dead,

Grey is the new sky blue,

Oh my dear,

I love you.


5. You used to take my breath away,

Now it's the air I breathe.


6. It's not you,

It's not me,

It's the smog.



Tanya, 'Love in the times of Delhi winters', aknownspace, 2020, 2, 6
